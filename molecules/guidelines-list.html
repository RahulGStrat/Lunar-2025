Please take note of the following before proceeding with your booking:

  • Reservation of new notes is only for SC Clients
  • SC Clients must have an active current or savings account for deduction of new notes exchange
  • Each client can only make 1 online booking
  • Ensure that your mobile number is updated in SCB's records. If not, you can login to SC Mobile App and update your mobile number under profile update
  • A confirmation SMS will be sent in 24 hours
  • No changes or cancellation may be made after booking is confirmed
  • Collection time slots and note denominations are on a first-come-first-serve basis
  • Unavailable slots will be greyed out to indicate that they are filled. Please pick another collection time

Please only collect your new notes on specified date, time and branch location. If you have missed your collection, your reservation request will automatically be cancelled. SCB seeks your cooperation to collect your new banknotes in an orderly manner.
