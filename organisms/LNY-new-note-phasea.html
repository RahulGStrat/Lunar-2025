Guidelines for New Notes Reservation & Collection

A step-by-step guide on reserving your new notes.

1

Visit Standard Chartered’s new notes reservation site.

2

Click on the “Reserve Now” button. Reservation window opens on 7 January, 12 a.m.

In the event of high traffic, you may be placed in a virtual waiting room.

3

Once you’ve reached the reservation form, enter your mobile number and email address.

Ensure the mobile number is a local mobile number for SMS communication purpose.

4

Select your preferred branch location, date and time for collection.

6

Verify your details and confirm your selection. You will receive a confirmation SMS and email after submitting your request.

Please take note of the following before proceeding with your booking:

  • Reservation of new notes is strictly for Standard Chartered customers with an active current or savings account for the new notes exchange. Please note that for non-Standard Chartered customers, your new notes booking will be declined at the branch.
  • Standard Chartered clients are limited to one online booking.
  • All reserved new notes will be debited/ deducted from the client’s current or savings account with Standard Chartered.
  • A confirmation SMS and email will be sent within 24 hours.
  • No changes or cancellations may be made after the booking has been confirmed.
  • Availability of collection time slots and note denominations are on a first-come-first-serve basis.
  • Greyed out slots means they have been taken up and are unavailable. Please select another collection time.

Please only collect your new notes on the specified date, time and branch location. If you have missed your collection, your reservation will be cancelled automatically. We seek your kind cooperation to follow the instructions stated herewith for the collection of your new notes.

7

Collect your notes on the date and time you have specified at the selected branch.

You will need to bring along your NRIC or any identification document and provide the reservation number indicated in the confirmation email or SMS sent to you at the branch. Please ensure that the debit account you wish to deduct from has sufficient funds.

Guidelines for New Notes Branch Walk-Ins

Clients aged 60 and above or clients who require special assistance can visit our branches for new notes exchange without prior reservation from 14 January to 28 January 2025 (subject to Branch operating hours).

*Please present your NRIC or Passport for identification for the new notes collection.
