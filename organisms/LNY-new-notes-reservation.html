Please take note of the following before proceeding with your booking:

  • Reservation of new notes is only for SC Clients. Please note that for non-SC Clients, there is a possibility that your booking may not be accepted at Branch. This can prevent you from collecting your order. We hope to seek your understanding.
  • SC Clients must have an active current or savings account for deduction of new notes exchange.
  • Each client can only make one online booking.
  • Ensure that your mobile number and email address is updated in SC's records. If not, you can login into SC Mobile App and update your mobile number and email address under 'profile update'.
  • A confirmation SMS and email will be sent in 24 hours.
  • No changes or cancellation may be made after booking is confirmed.
  • Collection time slots and note denominations are on a first-come-first-serve basis.
  • Unavailable slots will be greyed out to indicate that they are filled. Please pick another collection time

Guidelines for New Notes Branch Walk-Ins

Clients aged 60 and above or clients who require special assistance can visit our branches for new banknotes exchange without prior reservation from 14 January to 28 January 2025 (subject to Branch operating hours).

*Please present your NRIC or Passport for identification and SCB Debit Card for new notes collection.
