Personalise your e-Angbao

Send e-Angbaos to your loved ones and wish them a prosperous year

Step 1

Visit Standard Chartereds SC Mobile App.

Step 2

Once login, click on the eAngbao icon.

Step 3

After selecting the Payee, select send as e-Angbao.

Step 4

Customise your e-Angbao design and type a personalized message.

Step 5

Swipe to transfer and click on Share to send your e-Angbao to your love one.
